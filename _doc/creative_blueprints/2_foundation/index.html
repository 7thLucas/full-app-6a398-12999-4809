Why Siloam exists

The Problem

Quality and training teams review customer-service call recordings by hand to score agent performance. It's slow, inconsistent between reviewers, and almost impossible to track over time — so most calls are never scored and problems surface late.

The Solution

Siloam automates the whole loop: ingest a recording → transcribe & evaluate with AI → produce evidence-backed scores → recommend training → track remediation. Staff get answers without manual review; leads get dashboards and a closing feedback loop.

Core features, by who uses them

Service Agent Quality / Training Team Manager

Upload a recording

Submit a customer-service call — audio or video-with-audio. The evaluation runs end-to-end with no manual scoring step.

Scored evaluation

Per-category scores with issues sorted into clear severity bands — what went well, what needs work.

Evidence in the transcript

A full transcript with timestamps, so every score points back to the exact moment in the call.

Top-3 course recommendations

Targeted training suggestions tied to the weak spots, with an action plan to track remediation.

Quality teams & managers review evaluations, assign courses, and track performance trends through role-scoped dashboards — detailed on the next slides.
